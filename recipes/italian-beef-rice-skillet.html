Italian Beef and Rice Skillet

Description

This hearty Italian Beef and Rice Skillet is made in less than 30 minutes. I love recipes like this because they come together so easy. There is little preparation and even less dishes.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Mixed Veggies - Peas and Carrots are recommended, but any cariety would work too.
  • White Rice
  • Beef Broth
  • Seasonings - Oregano, Garlic, Basil, Salt and Pepper
  • Mozzerella Cheese - Shredded

    Steps

  1. Step 1: Brown Beef

    2. In a heated skillet, brown the ground beef until there is no more pink. Drain the excess fat out of the pan.

  2. Step 2: Cook the Rice

    3. Return the skillet to the stove and add in the rest of the ingredients except the mozzarella. Stir everything together. Bring the mixture to a light boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover and let everything cook until the rice is nice and tender.

    This should take about 17 minutes.

  3. Step 3: Finishing Touches

    4. Once everything is cooked, fluff up the rice with a fork and top off the skillet with the mozzarella cheese. For a melted effect, cover the pan for an extra 3 minutes. Top with chopped parsley if desired.

  4. Step 4: Serve/Store

    5. Serve or store in the freezer for up to 3 months.
