Sloppy Joes

Description

Sloppy Joes are loose meat sandwiches made with ground beef, peppers, and onions in a special tangy sauce. And thet special sauce is totally what makes them a sloppy Joe sandwich. Sloppy joe sauce is savory, tangy, and slightly sweet, similar to BBQ sauce, but less sweet and with a stronger tomato base

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tbsp. Olive Oil
  • 1 lbs. Ground Beef
  • 1 15oz. can Tomato Sauce
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 2 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 5 Hamburger Buns

    Steps

  1. Step 1: Dice Veggies

    2. Finely dice the onion and bell pepper, and mince the garlic.

  2. Step 2: Cook Beef

    3. Add the olive oil and beef to a skillet and cook over medium heat until the beef is cooked through. Drain off any excess fat.

  3. Step 3: Cook Veggies

    4. Add the onion, garlic, and bell pepper to the skillet with the beef. Continue to sauté until the onions are soft and translucent.

  4. Step 4: Add tomato sauce/paste, vinegar, ect

    5. Finally, add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, vinegar, brown sugar Dijon, chili powder, Worcestershire sauce,and salt to the skillet. Stir to combine.

  5. Step 5: Simmer

    6. Allow the meat and sauce to simmer over medium-low for about 5 minutes.

  6. Step 6: Serve

    7. Its mfing sloppy joe time
